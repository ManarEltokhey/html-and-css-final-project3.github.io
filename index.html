Vision

  As we continue to learn about animal behaviour, we envision a reality where animals'
  instinctual needs are respected and their lives are valued beyond serving a human purpose.


  We seek a cultural shift and a change in mindset; we'd like to see animal welfare become 
  a priority and this includes animals in homes and those that are free roaming and/or wild.




Mission

 
  pet shelter' mission is to improve animal welfare both in Egypt and beyond. 
  Education is a major component in achieving our goal and we've placed it at the forefront of our work.

  We want to further use our platform to generate awareness and provide useful information.
